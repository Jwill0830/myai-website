Go to Health tab → Fitness → Connected Devices → tap Apple Health. You'll be prompted to grant permissions. Once connected, your steps, sleep, heart rate, and workouts sync automatically.
Your Energy Score is a daily number from 0–100 that synthesizes your nutrition, sleep, HRV, recovery, and activity into a single signal. A score above 75 means you're well-recovered and fueled — a good day to push hard. Below 50 means your body needs rest or better nutrition. It updates throughout the day as you log meals and as wearable data comes in.
