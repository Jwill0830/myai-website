Help & Support

We typically respond within 24 hours.

Getting Started

Download MyAI from the App Store and create an account. Connect Apple Health or a wearable during onboarding for the best experience.

How do I log a meal?

Tell MyAI what you ate in plain English in the Chat tab. Example: "I had 3 eggs and an avocado for breakfast." MyAI calculates exact macros automatically.

How do I connect my wearable?

Go to Health → Fitness tab → tap any device. We support Apple Health, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Strava, and 400+ more devices.

What is the Energy Score?

Your Energy Score (0-100) combines nutrition, sleep, activity, and recovery into one daily number. Green (70+) means optimal. Orange (40-70) is moderate. Red (below 40) means rest and recover.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

MyAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MyAI application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address, name, and encrypted password
  • Health data: Nutrition logs, workout sessions, sleep data, steps, HRV, and wearable metrics you choose to share
  • Conversation data: Messages you send to MyAI to provide personalized responses and build your persistent memory profile
  • Device data: App usage patterns and crash reports to improve the service

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized AI coaching and health recommendations
  • To calculate your Energy Score and track progress toward your goals
  • To generate morning briefings and meal plans tailored to you
  • To build and maintain your persistent memory profile that improves over time

AI Processing & Memory

MyAI uses mem0 (a memory layer) and Qdrant (a vector database) to store and retrieve your personal context. This enables the AI to remember facts about you across conversations. Your memory data is stored in Qdrant Cloud with encryption at rest and in transit.

We use Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude APIs to process your messages and generate responses. Your messages are sent to these services as part of normal operation. We do not use your data to train these AI models.

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

Health data accessed through Apple HealthKit is used exclusively to provide MyAI's features. This data is:

  • Never used for advertising or marketing
  • Never shared with third parties for their own purposes
  • Never used to train AI models without explicit consent
  • Only used to power your personal health coaching experience

Wearable Data (ROOK Health)

We use ROOK Health SDK to integrate with wearable devices including Garmin, Whoop, Oura Ring, and Strava. ROOK acts as a data aggregation layer that syncs your health metrics to our platform. ROOK's own privacy policy governs their data handling practices.

Data Storage & Security

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security enabled
  • Memory: Qdrant Cloud vector database with encryption at rest
  • Cache: Upstash Redis with TLS encryption in transit
  • Passwords: SHA-256 hashed, never stored in plain text
  • Tokens: JWT with expiration, stored in iOS Secure Enclave

We Never

  • Sell your personal or health data to any third party
  • Use your health data for advertising purposes
  • Share your conversation history with other users
  • Access your data without your permission

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing privacy@myoperatingsystem.app or using the "Export my data" feature in the app.

Upon deletion request, we will remove your data from all systems within 30 days, including memory vectors in Qdrant, health records in our database, and cached data in Redis.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential data processing

California residents have additional rights under CCPA including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Upstash — Redis caching
  • Qdrant Cloud — vector memory storage
  • ROOK Health — wearable device integration
  • Nutritionix — food and nutrition database
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI language model (Claude)
  • Google — AI language model (Gemini)

Children's Privacy

MyAI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
