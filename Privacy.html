Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

The short version: your health data is yours. We encrypt it, we never sell it, and we never use it to train AI models. You can export or delete everything at any time.

Who we are

Solas is a personal AI health coach built by Jack Williams, based in Santa Ana, California. You can reach us at jack@trysolasapp.com.

What data we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Health and fitness data — meals, workouts, sleep, and other information you log through conversations with Solas
  • Wearable data — if you connect Apple Health, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, or Strava, we read the data those services provide (steps, HRV, sleep, recovery scores)
  • Conversation history — your chat messages with Solas, used to provide context and build your memory profile
  • Goals and preferences — dietary targets, fitness goals, coaching style preferences you set in the app
  • Usage data — basic app analytics (screens visited, features used) to improve the product. No advertising identifiers.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the Solas service:

  • To power your AI conversations and provide personalized coaching
  • To calculate your Energy Score and track your progress toward goals
  • To generate meal plans, grocery lists, and morning briefings
  • To build your persistent memory profile so Solas gets smarter over time
  • To send you notifications you've opted into (morning briefings)
  • To fix bugs and improve the app

What we never do

  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never use your health data to train AI models
  • We never share your data with advertisers
  • We never share identifiable data with any third party without your explicit consent, except as required by law

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in encrypted databases hosted on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

We use Upstash Redis for short-term caching and Qdrant for your memory vector database. All third-party infrastructure providers are bound by data processing agreements.

Third-party services

Solas uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • OpenAI / Google Gemini — AI language models that power your conversations. Messages are sent to these services to generate responses. Neither provider trains on your data under our agreements.
  • Nutritionix — food database used to calculate nutrition information from your meal descriptions
  • Apple HealthKit — if you grant permission, we read health data from your iPhone
  • Rook SDK — used to connect Garmin, Whoop, Oura, and Strava
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro subscriptions. We never see or store your card details.
  • Sentry — error tracking to help us fix bugs. Crash reports are anonymized.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Chat history older than 90 days may be automatically archived to reduce storage costs, but remains accessible and deletable on request.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Export — download your complete data at any time via Profile → Export My Data
  • Delete — request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correct — update or correct any information in your profile
  • Opt out — turn off any notifications or data collection features at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at jack@trysolasapp.com or use the in-app data export feature.

Children's privacy

Solas is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of Solas after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email jack@trysolasapp.com — we respond within 48 hours.
